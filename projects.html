CURRENT PROJECTS

BASIC RESEARCH PROJECTS

Identifying the conditions under which autoclitics may emerge

Exploring the environmental and behavioral conditions that lead to the development of autoclitic behavior in verbal organisms.

Using NLP to learn about the published behavior analysis literature

Applying natural language processing techniques to analyze and extract insights from the extensive body of behavior analysis research.

Quantitative analyses of the multiple control of choice

Developing mathematical models to understand how multiple environmental variables simultaneously control choice behavior.

Creating artificial organisms that imitate human decision-making

Building computational models that simulate human decision-making processes based on behavioral principles.

TRANSLATIONAL RESEARCH PROJECTS

Translating behavioral economic concepts to describe and predict ethical decision-making

Applying behavioral economic principles to understand and predict ethical choices in various contexts.

The practical utility of the quantitative models in non-laboratory environments

Testing and validating quantitative behavioral models in real-world settings outside of controlled laboratory conditions.

Integrating known and novel models of environment-behavior relations to improve clinical decision-making

Combining established and innovative models to enhance clinical interventions and treatment planning.

APPLIED RESEARCH PROJECTS

Alternative metrics and analytics for understanding IOA

Developing new approaches to measure and analyze interobserver agreement in applied settings.

Survey of Open Science Practices in Behavior Analysis

Investigating the adoption and implementation of open science practices within the behavior analysis community.

Perspectives of Behavior Analysts on the Role of AI in ABA

Exploring behavior analysts' views and experiences with artificial intelligence in applied behavior analysis.

Ethical AI for ABA

Developing ethical frameworks and guidelines for the responsible use of artificial intelligence in behavior analysis.
