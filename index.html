WELCOME TO THE BEHAVIORAL DATA SCIENCE RESEARCH LAB

Hello! Welcome to the webpage for one of the research labs at the Institute for Applied Behavioral Science at Endicott College!

Our research focuses primarily on: choice, decision-making, verbal behavior, and ethical behavior through the lens of behavioral economics and complex systems. Our research primarily leverages the methodologies of: human operant experiments, quantitative analyses of behavior-environment relations, machine learning, and computational modeling. All that is quite the mouthful so we shorten it to "behavioral data science".

Our lab tries to span the basic-translational-applied continuum, with findings from each area informing the others. Check out our Current Projects and Recent Publications pages for more information about our work. To learn more about the PI, check out his CV. Otherwise, feel free to check out the website to learn more about who we are and what we're getting into on a regular basis.
