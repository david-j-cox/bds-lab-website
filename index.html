WELCOME TO THE BEHAVIORAL DATA SCIENCE RESEARCH LAB

Hello! Welcome to the webpage for one of the research labs at the Institute for Applied Behavioral Science at Endicott College!

We research choice, decision-making, verbal behavior, and ethical behavior through the lens of behavioral economics and complex systems. We use human operant experiments, quantitative analyses of behavior-environment relations, machine learning, and computational modeling. The long name is shortened to "behavioral data science."

Our lab tries to span the basic-translational-applied continuum. Check out our Current Projects and Recent Publications pages for more information about our work. You can also check out his CV to learn more about the Principal Investigator (PI).
