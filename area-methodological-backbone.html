Methodological backbone

Running beneath every project is a common methodological spine. This area covers the quantitative, computational, and statistical methods we use to model environment-behavior relations, along with the research methodologies (e.g., measurement, design, and analysis) that let us see interactions in the first place and move findings from one level of analysis to the next.
